Description

Venusaur, Charizard, Blastoise, Pikachu, and many other Pokémon have been discovered on planet Earth!
Now’s your chance to discover and capture the Pokémon all around you—so get your shoes on, step outside, and explore the world. You’ll join one of three teams and battle for the prestige and ownership of Gyms with your Pokémon at your side.

Pokémon are out there, and you need to find them. As you walk around a neighborhood, your smartphone will vibrate when there’s a Pokémon nearby. Take aim and throw a Poké Ball… You’ll have to stay alert, or it might get away!

Search far and wide for Pokémon and items
Certain Pokémon appear near their native environment—look for Water-type Pokémon by lakes and oceans. Visit PokéStops, found at interesting places like museums, art installations, historical markers, and monuments, to stock up on Poké Balls and helpful items.

Catching, hatching, evolving, and more
As you level up, you’ll be able to catch more-powerful Pokémon to complete your Pokédex. You can add to your collection by hatching Pokémon Eggs based on the distances you walk. Help your Pokémon evolve by catching many of the same kind.

Take on Gym battles and defend your Gym
As your Charmander evolves to Charmeleon and then Charizard, you can battle together to defeat a Gym and assign your Pokémon to defend it against all comers.

It’s time to get moving—your real-life adventures await!

Customer Reviews

Your ableist discrimination is showing
     
by jabradford

The need for third party trackers is entirely your fault. All that ever spawns in my suburb are rats and pidgeys, and the occasional evee, and I'm sick of catching those. So I have to leave and drive closer to the city to find anything different. I'm disabled and can't drive myself places to do this, so I was relying on family and friends to drive me around hunting, and we had fun doing it. But now we can't do that anymore because you took away driving speed spawns? I have mild agoraphobia and the only thing that got me out of the house on a regular basis after 10 years was going for a drive to hunt Pokémon once or twice a week, and now I don't have that anymore because I can't walk and now I cant catch from the car. Your tracker problem has always been garbage and at least the third parties made it fun because I could find those shadow Pokémon and didn't have to drive up and down each street to hope to pick it up. You are being very ableist about the way your game is run, and I'm sad to see it become a thing of the past because it had really brightened up my summer to incentivize me to leave the house and spend time with family and friends like I hadn't in years. You ought to stop micromanaging and policing how people play your game and focus on new features that will keep people interested in it instead. But, it's your loss of revenue, and I'll certainly never play another Niantic game, or put money into one, either.

Lack of variety makes the game stagnant
     
by JEN25!

There are far to many updates with little improvements to each update. I understand that it is always going to be bugs and it will always be a work in progress. Suggestions to keep the game from being stagnant might be to release additional pokemon.( perhaps 5 with each update and varied by region) I've collected all available pokemon at this time and find the challenge and excitement gone. The additional incentive of this game is to get people out and about and explore. If the challenge is gone due to a lack of variety then the main goal is somewhat defeated. Another suggestion would be to receive more than 3 candies for Pokemon that are evolved and more than one candy for when the are transferred. Doesn't make sense that I catch an evolved Pokemon which takes 50 candies and only get 4 candies for catch and transfer. (Not to mention utilizing quite a few if not more ultra balls which are difficult to come by) Rural areas are impacted more with game play as stops and Pokemon variety seem far and few in-between.

Revised Update: your game has hit an all time low. The updates are garbage and the micromanaging has left this game uninteresting. The adventure that accompanied this app has been dissolved with the removal of third party tracking and the ability for capturing Pokémon as a passenger. The updates fail to give its users new Pokémon or other incentives to continue playing. Tired of the same old thing.

Overall I still like the game and all because it makes walking around a little less boring. Also is it too much to ask for a little more information from you to keep players invested? It feels like we can complain or try to help you be aware of bugs till the moon explodes and dont hear anything from you (I'm assuming Niantic can see these reviews if they wanted to). I've waited for a game like this since I was old enough to play pokemon and would hate to see something with so much potential die from missunderstandings and lack of feedback.
